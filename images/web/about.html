~Kim Namjoon AKA RM~

This is Kim Namjoon (김남준). His stage name is RM (아르엠), but was formally Rap Monster (랩몬스터). He is the leader of one of the most successful boy bands in the world,BTS. He is also the main rapper of the boy band, songwriter, compuser, and producer. His birthday is September 12,1994. He is a Virgo and his MBTI type is ENTP. His height is 5'11 (181cm).
Namjoon was born in Seoul (Sangdo-dong), Korea. He has one sibling which is his younger sister Kim Kyung Min. Before he debuted, he was an underground rapper known as Runch Ransa, and he was a part of the Daenamhyup crew. He is the youngest of the Hyung line and he was the first member to join BTS in 2010 when it was in the process of forming.
Namjoon uses his stage name RM for his individual work. He has two mixtapes. The first is "RM" which was released on March 20,2015. And his second one "Mono" which was released on October 23, 2018. While BTS is on break, he is possibly in the process of making his own album like the other BTS members. But it is known that he is traveling and visiting art museums.

~Seok Jin AKA Jin~

BTS member Jin was born in Anyang, Gyeonggi-do but his family moved to Gwacheon, Gyeonggi-do (South Korea) when he was a baby. Jin is 29 years old and he is the oldest member of BTS. He attended Konkuk University in Seoul where he graduated with a major in theater and film. Jin is the sub-vocalist of BTS (he is good at high notes in their songs) and he writes some of the group's lyrics.
Although Jin's main skills aren't dancing he has won praise as a choreographer from both his fans and his bandmates. He also knows how to play the piano, pipe organ and a bit of guitar. Jin received an offer from SM Entertainment when he was in middle school, he passed the first audition but thought it might be a scam and decided not to go any farther with the offer. He was later on scouted in his college days by Big Hit Entertainment for an acting role, but the company made him BTS trainee instead which lead to him being part of the group.
Jin is also known for his athletic gifts, he is good at snowboarding, tennis and golf, he even managed to do well at wakeboarding (on his first try!) on a local TV show. Jin thought of being a journalist in his first year of high school, but then changed his mind to acting after being moved by the acting in the Korean drama "Queen Seondeok". Although Jin eventually became a K-pop idol to diversify his experiences, he thinks that he would have been a farmer if he wasn't a singer since he used to work at his uncle's melon and strawberry farm. Jin was first to launch his own show "Eat Jin Live" on V's Live BTS channel. Jin is the cook in the group and often shares his cooking through his blog and the V app.

BTS member Jungkook was born in Busan, South Korea. He is 24 years old and is the youngest member of BTS. Jungkook attended the School of Performing Arts Seoul which caused him to start high school a year later than others because of his early debut in the music career. Jungkook joined BTS when he was only 15 years old, he is the main vocalist, sub-rapper and lead dancer of the group.

Jungkook is also into photography and video editing, he actually released a series of travel videos called the Golden Closet Films (GCF) which he filmed himself.
Jungkook is nicknamed Golden Maknae which means "golden youngest” since his mother dreamed of gold before he was born. Jungkook really appreciates the BTS fandom, he has a tattoo saying ARMY (the BTS fandom name and also the members' initials) on his right knuckles, he also has a tattoo of the BTS ARMY logo on his hand and a crown on his finger meaning that his fans are kings. Before joining BTS he auditioned in a program called “Super Star K Season 3” in 2011,he never made in to the finals but got casting offers from seven agencies, including Big Hit Entertainment (the agency that got him and other to form BTS). Jungkook is the fourth member to join BTS.
